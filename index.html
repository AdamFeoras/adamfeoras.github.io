About Me

Welcome, thank you for the visit. My name is Matt, and I'm a writer. When I was a freshman in high school I rewrote "The Cask of Amontillado" for a school assignment and things got out of hand.

I love the creative outlet that writing affords me. It's not all I do with my free time, but it's pretty close. I took a break from writing to update this About Me section, and when I'm done, I'll start writing again and won't stop until I go to bed tonight. So if you want a wordsmith who's passionate and talented, your search is over; way to go.

In addition to being a writer, I'm also a reader (obviously), a web developer, a hiker, a beachcomber, a cook, a gamer, an insomniac, and a cat person.

I always like to meet new people. I like to get commissions, too. I especially like job offers. So don't hesitate to contact me. Click here to have a look at my CV and contact information.

Please note that I'm redoing my website right now. It shouldn't take more than a week or two as I consider what direction I want to take my career in, but please be aware this is not its final form. Check back shortly.

Capricorn

I'm coding one of those old-school text-based adventure games that were all the rage when I was a kid. Domenico is a former merchant who has stepped out of retirement following the death of his wife and is dabbling in illicit maritime activities. The late fifteenth century Amalfi Coast is beautiful, but capricious. I've posted a simplified sandbox version that you can play right in your browser. Click here to have a look.

Dane Balcon, Tiefling Fighter

Like most tieflings, Dane has lived a lonely life of passive persecution.

Recently, his wards, the Siannodel family, were killed in a raid. They were good to him and treated him like a close friend of the family and their death has sent him into a tailspin. It's not that he blames himself so much as that he's lost; they were his anchor in life, the source of his sense of purpose, the only people in the world who loved him.

The Siannodels instilled in Dane a sense of loyalty that serves as his moral compass. In a world that's thoughtlessly hostile to you, your friends come before almost all other considerations.
